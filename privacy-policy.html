Privacy Policy

Humanity United for Universal Demilitarisation (HUFUD) is committed to being fully compliant with the policies of the European Commission's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
What information do we collect?
We collect information from you when you subscribe to HUFUD emails, register to an event, or donate money to us. As appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name and e-mail address. We use this information to tell you about the latest HUFUD news, events, and publications.
How do we protect your information
We hold only the minimal amount information required in order to perform the services that our users expect to receive. We ensure that any data that we hold is adequately secure and that any data that is no longer required is promptly deleted. Our users have the right to check the information we hold about them and to correct and/or request deletion of personal information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information.
Third Party Service Providers
Our site uses third party service providers, including Mailchimp for our mailing list and Eventbrite for events that take place in venues without a designated box office. These third party services have separate and independent terms and conditions and privacy practices. We therefore do not accept any responsibility or liability for their policies. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these service providers.
